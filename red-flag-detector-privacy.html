Red Flag Detector Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Eponymical Games Ltd ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use Red Flag Detector.

Red Flag Detector is an entertainment-only joke app. It is not a personality, relationship, health, identity, safety or biometric identification service.

1. Who we are

Eponymical Games Ltd is a private limited company registered in England and Wales, based at 86-90 Paul Street, London, EC2A 4NE, United Kingdom. Contact: direct@eponymicalgames.com.

2. Data collected by the app

The current Red Flag Detector TestFlight candidate does not collect personal data from you. It does not require an account, does not connect to our backend, does not include analytics and does not include a live ad network.

3. Photos and face signals

The app lets you take a photo or choose one from your photo library so it can generate an unserious red or green flag result. Photos are intended to stay on your device. When available, the app may use local on-device face detection to read simple face signals for entertainment-only scoring. These signals are not used to identify you, are not uploaded to us, are not stored by us and are not shared by us.

4. Sharing results

If you tap the app's share button, Red Flag Detector uses your operating system's native share sheet. The destination app or service you choose handles the shared image under its own privacy terms.

5. Permissions

  • Camera - used only when you choose to take a photo.
  • Photo library - used only when you choose to import a photo.

You can manage these permissions in your device settings.

6. Tracking, advertising and analytics

The current TestFlight candidate does not track you across apps or websites, does not request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission, does not include a live third-party ad SDK and does not collect analytics.

7. Data retention

Because the current app candidate is designed not to upload or collect your photos, face signals or results, we do not retain those items on our servers. Local temporary files are handled on your device by the app and operating system.

8. Children

The app is intended for a general audience and entertainment use. It is not directed at children under the age where parental consent is required by applicable law.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date above.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact direct@eponymicalgames.com.
