Red Flag Detector Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Eponymical Games Ltd ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use Red Flag Detector.

Red Flag Detector is an entertainment-only joke app. It is not a personality, relationship, health, identity, safety or biometric identification service.

1. Who we are

Eponymical Games Ltd is a private limited company registered in England and Wales, based at 86-90 Paul Street, London, EC2A 4NE, United Kingdom. Contact: direct@eponymicalgames.com.

2. Data collected by the app

Red Flag Detector does not collect personal data for us. It does not require an account, does not connect to our backend and does not include analytics. The app includes the Google Mobile Ads SDK and optional in-app purchases; those are described in sections 6 and 7 below.

3. Photos, chat screenshots and face signals

The app lets you take a photo or choose an image from your photo library — including a screenshot of a conversation — so it can generate an unserious red or green flag result. Images are analyzed on your device and are not uploaded to us. When available, the app may use local on-device face detection to read simple face signals, and local on-device text recognition to read the text visible in a screenshot, in both cases only to generate the entertainment verdict. These signals and texts are not used to identify you, are not uploaded to us, are not stored by us and are not shared by us.

4. Sharing results

If you tap the app's share button, Red Flag Detector uses your operating system's native share sheet. The destination app or service you choose handles the shared image under its own privacy terms.

5. Permissions

  • Camera - used only when you choose to take a photo.
  • Photo library - used only when you choose to import a photo.

You can manage these permissions in your device settings.

6. Advertising

The app may show banner and interstitial ads served through the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). The app requests non-personalized ads and does not request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission. Like any ads SDK, Google may process device information (such as device identifiers, IP address and coarse ad-interaction data) to serve ads and prevent fraud; see Google's Privacy Policy. In test builds only Google test creatives are shown. You can remove all ads with the one-time Remove Ads purchase.

7. In-app purchases

The optional Remove Ads purchase is processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details. The app keeps only a local on-device flag that ads are disabled; purchases can be restored through your store account.

8. Data retention and deletion

Because the app does not upload or collect your photos, chats, face signals or results, we do not retain those items on our servers. Local temporary files are handled on your device by the app and operating system. The app's settings include a "Delete my data" action that removes all locally stored app data (session counters and preferences); uninstalling the app removes everything.

8. Children

The app is intended for a general audience and entertainment use. It is not directed at children under the age where parental consent is required by applicable law.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date above.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact direct@eponymicalgames.com.
