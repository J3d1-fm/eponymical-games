Your photos and chats stay on your device.

Crush Check processes the raw image, visible chat text and simple face signals locally. This content and the ML Kit recognition inputs and outputs stay on your device and are not uploaded to Eponymical Games or Google. Google ML Kit may separately collect limited operational telemetry such as device and app information, a per-install identifier, performance and utilization data, API configuration and event or error metrics. The app may also show Google Mobile Ads and uses Google's consent flow where required. Read the Privacy Policy for the exact ML Kit, advertising and purchase disclosures.

Support

Need help?

Email direct@eponymicalgames.com. Include your device model, operating-system version and a short description of what happened. Do not email private photos or chat screenshots unless you deliberately choose to share them with support.

Why do I have to choose an age range?

Crush Check is for ages 13 and older. The neutral first-launch gate asks only for under 13, 13–17 or 18+, not your date of birth. Under-13 users are blocked. Your selected range stays on this device; Google receives only the under-age-of-consent treatment flag needed for the correct advertising and consent treatment.

Why does Crush Check ask for camera or photo access?

Camera access is used only when you choose to take a photo. Photo-library access is used only when you choose to import an image. You can change either permission in device Settings.

How do I remove ads?

Open Crush Check Settings and choose the one-time Remove Ads purchase. The price is shown by your app store before you confirm.

How do I restore Remove Ads?

Use Restore Purchases in the app while signed in to the same app-store account that made the purchase.

Where can I change advertising privacy choices?

If Google requires a privacy-options entry for your region, open Crush Check Settings and choose Privacy choices. You can also limit advertising identifiers in your device privacy settings.

How do I delete local app data?

Use Delete my data in Crush Check Settings or uninstall the app. This removes the saved age range together with other local app data. App-store purchase records are managed by Apple or Google and are not deleted by this local action.
