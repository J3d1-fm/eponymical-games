    processed locally and are not sent to Eponymical Games or Google through
    the advertising SDK;
  - Google Mobile Ads may process coarse location, device or other identifiers,
    advertising data, product interaction, crashes, performance and other
    diagnostics for ads, measurement, security, fraud prevention and SDK
    operation;

  - a Privacy choices entry is available in app Settings when required;
  - the one-time Remove Ads purchase is handled by the app store and can be
    restored from the same store account.
